Manufaktura

In the oldest part of Belgrade, in Dorćol, there is Manufaktura, a restaurant of authentic Balkan cuisine and drinks. In the ambience that combines traditionally and modernly, you can expect from barbecues, grilled meat, specialties under the honeycomb and delicious delicacy, such as the grandma's kitchen.

Madera

Madera's restaurant for decades is an inevitable and cultic place of the capital. It is located at the address of Kralja Aleksandra No. 43, leaning against the Tasmajdan park, it was always a day-long stay of "sighted" Belgradeians: legends of Belgrade, artists, athletes, politicians, businessmen, public figures, Maderu restaurant are perceived as another house.

Toro Latin Gastro Bar
