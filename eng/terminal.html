Terminal

The Gastro Bar Terminal is located on the Vračar plateau, in the immediate vicinity of the Temple of St. Sava, with an exceptional position and a beautiful reflection of the illuminated Temple during the night, in a business-residential building of contemporary architecture, conceptually confronting and connecting traditional and modern.

Terminal Gastro Bar offers not only top-quality food and drinks but also the whole atmosphere, resembling gastronomic bars in the world's major metropolises. Looking after what attracts the guests primarily, and paying great attention to what animates and keeps them, we have focused the concept towards entertainment and the overall experience. Live music, superb sound, unusual and unconfined seating arrangement guarantee unforgettable time. We can also boast our own bakery, pastry shop, juice bar, cocktail bar and pizzeria.
