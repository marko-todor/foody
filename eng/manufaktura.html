Manufaktura

Restaurant in downtown Belgrade, interesting concept, authentic. Restaurant Manufaktura in Kralja Petra Street is one of the places that remain permanently in memory. An unusual interior that imposes an optional and relaxed atmosphere, a very specific food that is served in a way that is not so common. Perhaps more importantly, all the foodstuffs used to prepare meals come from our climate, from organic farming. Great wines and brandy are also from the best domestic, private winery and distilleries, and all this can be bought at the restaurant itself.

Grilled specialties, buns, directly from the grill, the most famous pikanteries of Bulgarian, Greek and Macedonian cuisine, homemade treats originating from the best gastronomic tradition of our area ... Belgrade's restaurants are famous for the great offer of such ducks, but rarely all that is nice unified and presented as a Manufaktura restaurant.
