Smokvica

One of the most beautiful gardens, which has been popular among Belgrade for years, is Smokvica. Quiet, hidden, tucked into Vračar's streets, it's just a loud clatter. Here you meet the urban charm of Belgrade. If you want to go to Provence in your mind, you know where to come by, but only on coffee or lemon, possibly a quick breakfast, which is fine for painting.

Smokvica is well positioned in one of the oldest districts of Belgrade, near Kalemgdan, Knez Mihajlova Street, surrounded by interesting cultural and historical institutions and unique Dorćol sites, Smokvica is the right place for a group of young people to travel through the Serbian capital.
