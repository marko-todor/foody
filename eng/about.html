Foody

Foody is a service for online ordering and delivery of food in Serbia. Popular restaurants and cafes are united in one place to make it easy for you to order food. It works simply: enter your address to see which restaurants are delivering to you. Enjoy Foody and order food quickly and efficiently every day.

Why foody?

Great choice of restaurants

Hundreds of restaurants have menus on the site. Choose a restaurant, compare prices, read reviews.

No hidden costs

Prices on the site are the same as in restaurants. Foody policy is not to take a commission from the users.

No phone calls

Online ordering is safe and convenient. No more phone calls and misunderstandings.

User rewards

By ordering over Foody, you collect tokens that you can pay for in future foods.

Honest comments

Foody does not delete negative comments. Therefore, you can make your own decision more easily and objectively.

Group orders

Order with friends! If you invite your friends to join Foody you get free tokens.
