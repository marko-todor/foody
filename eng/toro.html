Toro Latin Gastro Bar

One of the best worldwide chefs Ričard Sandoval, in cooperation with restaurant Small factory of taste, has opened his first restaurant in Europe, and exactly in Belgrade - Toro Latin Gastrobar, which is located in Concrete Hall. Toro Latin Gastrobar is based on concept "small plates".It means that every dish is cut into small pieces, so everyone could try everything.

The kitchen is Latin American with the influence of Asian, and all dishes are prepared from fresh meat and vegetables, with minimal use of fat. Sandoval personally, from the farm in Zlatibor, chooses the meat to be served, and all the meat is prepared in this local in the Josper Grill. Here, as an appetizer, gumacamola and smoked trout with tortilla chips are recommended. Among the other specialties are the chifa style fried rice, beefsteak on the spit (in the Peruvian marinade) and the ocopa sauce (peanut sauce, milk, biscuits, hot peppers and black and white onions), cocas style flat bread, zardande style - Mexican style fish grilled, hot-dog toro, toro glass ...
